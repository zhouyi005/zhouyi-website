About Me

Zhouyi Zuo is a pianist currently pursuing a Master of Music in Piano Performance at the University of Kansas. He received his Bachelor's degree from Soochow University, ranking among the top 10 students. He is a prize winner in numerous competitions including the Steinway National Youth Piano Competition and KMTA Piano Competition.

He has been invited to perform in festivals, masterclasses, and special concerts across the U.S. and China. His performances have been praised for their exquisite control and expressive depth.
